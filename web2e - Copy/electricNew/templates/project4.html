Project Details

This project involved a comprehensive electrical panel upgrade to improve safety and increase power capacity for a residential property.

  • Client: MR. AKAFAC IVO
  • Industry: Residential Electrical
  • Services Provided:
    • 200-amp panel upgrade
    • Circuit breaker replacement
    • New grounding system installation
    • Safety inspection and certification
  • Technology Used: Modern Circuit Breakers, Smart Panel Components, Safety Equipment
  • Duration: 3 days
  • Team Size: 2 Licensed Electricians

The main objectives of this panel upgrade project were:

  • Increase electrical capacity to meet modern power demands
  • Replace outdated circuit breakers with modern safety features
  • Install proper grounding system
  • Ensure compliance with current electrical codes
  • Improve overall electrical safety of the property

Benefits of the Upgrade

  • Increased electrical capacity for modern appliances and devices
  • Enhanced safety features with modern circuit breakers
  • Improved energy efficiency
  • Better protection against electrical hazards
  • Updated to meet current electrical codes
  • Potential reduction in insurance costs

Project Location

The panel upgrade was completed in Limbe, Cameroon, located in WOVIA close to the sea.

Project completion date: 2024
