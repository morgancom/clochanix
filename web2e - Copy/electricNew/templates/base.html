Electrical Tips & Safety Guide

Essential knowledge for electrical safety and efficiency

Safety Tips

  • Always turn off power before electrical work
  • Use certified surge protectors
  • Keep electrical panels accessible
  • Never overload outlets

Maintenance Tips

  • Regular inspection of cords and plugs
  • Clean electrical fixtures regularly
  • Test circuit breakers monthly
  • Check for warm outlets or switches

Energy Efficiency

  • Switch to LED lighting
  • Use smart power strips
  • Install motion sensors
  • Regular appliance maintenance
